Interesting facts about Panda

  • Bamboo is critical to their diet
  • They have great camouflage for their environment
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues
  • Chameleons are adapted for climbing and visual hunting

Interesting facts about Cicada

  • They’re underground builders
  • They Have One of the Longest Insect Life Spans
  • Cicada can live for up to 17 years in the larva stage!
